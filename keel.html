Who I work with

Keel Systems is for people stuck between “do nothing” and “hire a full-time IT department.”

  • A small company where IT is “whoever knows the Wi-Fi password.”
  • A home user with a growing lab who wants it to stop feeling fragile.
  • A team that outgrew a single router and a shared inbox and does not know what to fix first.

What I can help you with

Small business networks and Wi-Fi

  • Clean, labeled layout (modem, router, switches, APs, VLANs when needed).
  • Reliable Wi-Fi coverage with a clear SSID strategy and guest access when it makes sense.
  • Firewall rules and remote access that do not require a CCNA to maintain.

Microsoft 365 and SharePoint

  • Move shared drives into SharePoint with a structure that matches how you work.
  • Permissions that reduce “everyone has access to everything” situations.
  • Simple Power Automate flows for approvals, notifications, and recurring tasks.

Home labs and self-hosted services

  • Proxmox, containers, and a reasonable number of VMs instead of an unmanageable zoo.
  • Nextcloud, Jellyfin, and other services behind sane DNS, certificates, and authentication.
  • Tailscale or WireGuard-style access that is secure and low friction.

Lightweight automation and reporting

  • PowerShell and Python scripts to reduce repetitive tasks and manual reporting.
  • Exports and simple dashboards that give clarity without needing a BI team.

Example engagements

Local business with scattered files

Files spread across USB drives, random Google accounts, and a dusty office PC. We map what you actually use, pick a sensible platform, and migrate with clear structure and permissions.

Resort or campground with shaky Wi-Fi

Point-to-point links, consumer routers, and a lot of guesswork. We document the topology, simplify it, and improve coverage and stability so guests and staff can just get online.

Home power user with a growing lab

A server or two, services everywhere, and the feeling that one mistake could knock everything over. We tidy the layout, add backups, and clean up DNS and access.

“We need IT, but not full-time”

A small team that wants recurring help with upkeep, projects, and decisions. We create a short roadmap, fix the biggest risks, then keep it stable with simple runbooks.

How a typical project runs

Every environment is different, but the flow stays predictable so you always know what is next.

1

Short intake

You tell me what hurts today, what gear you already have, and what “fixed” would look like.

2

Map and plan

I map the current setup, propose a practical target state, and we confirm scope and priorities.

3

Build and clean up

We implement changes in a controlled way, with backups and rollback paths where it matters.

4

Docs and handoff

You get diagrams, notes, and runbooks so you are not dependent on me forever.
