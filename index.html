Keel Systems

Keel Systems is my consulting practice for small businesses and teams who want clear workflows and infrastructure they actually understand. I come in when things “kind of work” but nobody can explain how, map the real processes, and turn them into flowcharts, decision gates, and KPIs that work for you instead of against you.

From there we translate the maps into practical IT:

  • Self-hosted and cloud stacks using tools like Proxmox, Nextcloud, and Jellyfin, fronted by Traefik and Cloudflare Tunnels.
  • Network segmentation, firewall policy, and secure remote access with tools such as Tailscale.
  • Lightweight automation for backups, media, and reporting using PowerShell and Python.

Lifestyle and training

A few years ago I dropped a significant amount of weight and built my strongest powerlifting numbers while working full time. Then life, stress, and priorities shifted and the habits that got me there slipped away.

I am now rebuilding those habits with a calmer, systems-first approach: sensible nutrition, strength training that fits around work and family, and routines that I can sustain rather than white-knuckle. The goal is to prove to myself that I can do it again and keep it this time.

Longer term I would like to help regular people set up realistic training and nutrition frameworks, especially those balancing demanding jobs and family. If you want to be on the radar for that work, reach out and I will keep you in the loop as it takes shape.
